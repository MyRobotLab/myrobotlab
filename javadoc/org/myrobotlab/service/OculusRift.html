Class OculusRift

  • All Implemented Interfaces:
    java.io.Serializable, java.lang.Runnable, ServiceInterface

    public class OculusRift
extends Service
    The OculusRift service for MyRobotLab. Currently this service only exposed the head tracking information from the rift. The Yaw, Pitch and Roll are exposed. Yaw - twist around vertical axis (look left/right) Pitch - twist around horizontal axis (look up/down) Roll - twist around axis in front of you (tilt head left/right) Coming soon, lots of great stuff...
